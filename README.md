## About



I’m **Sai Prasad**, a Computer Science graduate and **CompTIA Security+ certified** cybersecurity professional, currently learning and actively seeking **entry-level roles in SOC, Cybersecurity Analyst, and Blue Team–focused positions**.



I believe cybersecurity is built through **hands-on practice**, not theory alone. I spend my time building home labs, working on security projects, and solving real-world scenarios on platforms like TryHackMe to understand how attacks actually happen and how they can be detected early.



My approach is practical and workflow-driven. I explore attacker techniques to understand behavior, then switch into a **defender’s mindset** — focusing on **SIEM monitoring, log analysis, threat detection, and basic incident response**. This helps me think like a SOC analyst, not just a learner.



---



## My Security Approach



- I learn security by **doing**, not memorizing  

- I break things in labs to understand **real attack paths**  

- I focus on **logs, alerts, and evidence**, not just tools  

- I write small **automation scripts** to support detection  

- I document what I learn through **projects and blogs**
